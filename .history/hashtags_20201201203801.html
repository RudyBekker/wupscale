    Wealth Wednesday #2 Hashtags

    Jun 17, 2020
    Hello everyone! It is Wealth Wednesday!
    Today we are talking about hashtags.
    I see many people have problems with them, or just throw a few here and there, and wonder why they have no results. There is a few things you can do to perfect them, if interested, continue reading!

    1. Do your research!
    You have seen this many times without anyone actually telling you what to do.
    You have to be active, and find accounts in your niche with the same size as yours. It is obviously important that they should be performing well, growing (organically!) without fake followers. If thats happening, they are doing something right!
    Why do they have to be the same size(followers) as you?
    The same hashtags wont work for a profile with 1M followers and 150 followers.
    If someone is growing in your niche go and copy their hashtags(easier on a pc) and test them in your posts!

    2. Test, test, test!
    This is an important step in marketing whether talking about ads, hashtags or content. Having a copied hashtag list isnt enough, you have to tweak it and find the best performing ones!
    For me it was almost 100 posts to test, but now I have 3 groups that will surely explode me to the explore page.

    3.Have multiple hashtag groups
    I have atleast 3-5 general hashtags in my niche, with the size of 20-25 and the rest is related to the current post. Instagram gets tired of you use the same hashtags over and over, and your posts will eventually lose reach.

    4.How many hashtags to use?
    You can use up to 30 hashtags each post,and I recommend to reach the limit, or atleast around 25. You should have 3 groups generally:
    10 hashtags around 500k-1M size, general niche hashtags.
    Then 10 other hashtags again in your niche, a little more specific with the size under 500k.
    And the rest should be related to the current post around the size of 100k. This will get you rank on the smaller hashtags, and start a snowball towards the big ones.

    5.Best research strategy
    Go and count the average likes on your posts, and then you can find the best performing hashtags for your page. If you usually have 100likes, you should look for small hashtags (around 100k) and check the top nine posts and average their like counts. If its similar to your page's then that hashtag is likely to work for you.

    I hope these tips can help you up your hashtag game! Ask in the comments or shoot a message if you need some help!

This way with Telegram, you don't feel like you are wasting your time, it's more like an investment. 3. There are more options, it's faster to join them, you can literally find the perfect niche group for your page with the desired engagement numbers you need. 4. You don't risk an account ban. We know that Instagram is monitoring these groups, of course they are avare of these "grey methods". That is why most groups restrict the words "new post", "like,comment,save", and post links.     Wealth Wednesday #4 Action Block

    01 Jul, 2020 •
    Hello Everyone! Here is another (I hope) helpful post, now about Action Block. If you don't know what action block is, it is when Instagram blocks you from commenting, liking, and writing captions. You can still post a picture without a caption, share stories, and use the DMs. It happens because you exceed the action limits on Instagram. That is why aggressive follow for follow, automation, spam liking and mass-unfollowing doesn't really work. Also, be careful with liking comments, that's also a sensitive point. There are two types of action block, one with an expiry date, and one without, it can even last up to two weeks. What are the limits? It depends on your account age, engagement rates, and follower count. The younger your account is, the less action you can do. I have just basic numbers that protected me from getting banned, which is MAX: 20 follow/unfollow every 2hours 1000 likes daily 30 comments and comment likes every 1 hour 3 post a day (doesn't get you banned but no point in posting more) How to lift the action block? If you still get banned there are a few ways you can try to get rid of it. Obviously, if it has an expiry date you can't help that, but otherwise, try these tips: 1. Clear the Instagram app cache Usually, you can do it by going to you phone's settings, Apps, select Instagram and there should be this option. 2. Relog Too obvious to be true, but it worked for me. Just log out of your account and then log in and wait a bit. 3. Re-install the Instagram app It basically step 1 and 2 combined- 4. Change your bio, stay active Instagram might banned you because you looked too spammy, so changing up you bio and profile picture might be a good way to show that you are an active user. Also, keep sharing stories and talking to your audience, again, showing that you are a real, engaging Instagram user, not a spammer bot. 5. Report the problem to Instagram If you really feel like you didn't do anything and ended up being action blocked, report the problem to Instagram through the app settings. When the “action blocked” error pops up, it usually has two options: Tell us and Ignore. To request a manual review by Instagram, tap the Tell us button. But some types of error pop-ups have no other option but OK. In this case, do the following: press Settings, then Help, then Report a Problem. (Use this even if you used some services) 6. Connect your Instagram account to a Facebook page This action again shows Instagram that you are not a bot. 7. Wait Sometimes none of these tips work, then you can't do anything but wait up to 2 weeks. Try to keep you audience engaged through stories and DMs. It can really feel devastating not being able to post for 2 weeks, but try to stay patient and not do actions that just pop up the Block message, as it won't help you. Some of these tips might help you, but be careful, as too many action blocks can get your account banned. When you get back your account, make sure you slowly ramp things up, not risking another block. I hope this can help you! If you have any questions let us know, feel free to contact us!
