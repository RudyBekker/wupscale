    Wealth Wednesday #10 Build a community!

    By Máté Hunyor • 25 Aug, 2020 •
    Happy Wealth Wednesday Everyone! It's been a while, but while missing posts I have also been testing some new strategies, and I one of them seem to be really working, so read along to learn more. To us "experts" or people who take Instagram as a serious business it might be a surprise, but sticking to it's roots and handling it as a social platform actually still gets you loyal followers. There are more than just hashtag research, spamming robots, and buying fake followers. If you can build your own community, the results might be slower, but in the long term you can only win. Blablalbla..So what to actually do? To start this you SHOULD have atleast 2000-3000 followers. It is about making your followers engage with your page. You have to build a community, but no one works for free, neither do your followers. You have to reward them for helping you grow, and with a solid following a shoutout is a good deal. I started off testing by pinning the best comments. My caption was: "Caption this,best 3 comments get pinned!" And people actually wrote more engaging and well thought out comments, so they could get pinned and more people can see their profiles. (Yes people mostly care about themselves, but it is fine, we make a deal!) So after this short test I went ahead and created my own hashtag that had 0 posts before, and started to promote it in my bio,story and captions: "Use #myhashtag and tag @us in your posts to be featured!" So why does this work? As I have already mentioned, people like publicity and exes on their profiles, so a free shoutout in exchange to adding an extra hashtag and tag is really simple. This is why you need atleast 2000 followers, to make your story reshares more valuable. These days I go ahead and check my hashtag and posts I am tagged in, and share the best ones to my stories, and even post a few of them. How do you get more followers by this? By being tagged in photos, everyone who sees that picture is likely to come by your profile, maybe even click through and check out your page. If you have a well built profile, that's a new follower! Imagine being tagged in only 5 posts a day, but each post reaches atleast 200 people. That's 1000 new potential profile visits! Of course, in reality the conversion rate is really low, but this way lets you build a solid connection with your fanbase, and gets you new, interested followers! Also eventually your own hashtag will be also trending, that would work the same way as I just described the tags. So please! Use this social media platform as it was meant to be used! Let's build a strong community and grow further! Have an amazing rest of your day!

Phlanx Extremely useful tool to quickly and freely check your Engagement rate.     Wealth Wednesday #8 Telegram engagement groups

    29 Jul, 2020 •
    Hello Everyone! What a beautiful day, Wealth Wednesday #8 is here! Even though it's more like #7.5. This is part 2 of the engagement group post, and this time we got into more details about Telegram groups. I will link the previous post so you can read what engagement groups are and if they worth using. Click here to read! One big difference is, while IG groups can only have 32 members, Telegram groups can have up to thousands of members! This is definitely a good thing, as more active members means faster engagement on your posts. How to get in these groups? First, you have to download the Telegram app, and register simply in one minute. Then, you hit the search bar and type " *your niche* dx" This should bring up a few groups. DX means how many times you have to engage before you can drop your link. You will get the same amount of likes back. There are likes only, like+comment groups, niche specific ones and even with minimum follower requirements. You can literally type Telegram engagement groups into google and you will have plenty of sources to choose from. I recommend starting out with the "Boostgram" family. Now, that you are in these groups you usually have to write /list to get the list of the posts you need to engage with. In comment groups it's usually comments that contain atleast 4 words, and are genuine, and related to the photo. Some groups even have you following their admins. After you are done engaging on all your links, you go back to the group and write: DX *number you had to engage with* **yourlink** Make sure to format your link correctly, you have to remove everything until the question mark like this: https://www.instagram.com/p/CDPCnyyghU6/?igshid=1udkei3t8wisn ------------> https://www.instagram.com/p/CDPCnyyghU6/ Personally, I think Telegram group are better for the following reasons: 1. It's much more faster, you get the engagement a lot sooner compared to an Intagram POD. 2. This should have been number one! The bot makes sure for every engagement you get one back! We all know that in IG groups some people just drop their links, but never engage. Even if they do, it's so generic or just an emoji. This way with Telegram, you don't feel like you are wasting your time, it's more like an investment. 3. There are more options, it's faster to join them, you can literally find the perfect niche group for your page with the desired engagement numbers you need. 4. You don't risk an account ban. We know that Instagram is monitoring these groups, of course they are avare of these "grey methods". That is why most groups restrict the words "new post", "like,comment,save", and post links. Basically these are the reasons to use Telegram, but I think it is enough to switch to them from IG groups. If you think I forgot something, or have questions, or a different perspective make sure to comment and give your feedback! Have a great day!

    22 Jul, 2020 •
    Hello everyone! Hope you had a great weekend! A bit of a later post this week, I was working so much that I slipped a bit... Anyway, at least there is another ebook coming really soon! Last week we talked about engagement rates and fake profiles, so today it's a great opportunity to learn about boosting your engagement rate, and with that boosting your credibility and reach. Obviously, the first step is to create and post engaging content. On Instagram, the "content is king" rule is still true! Post great, well-written captions with questions, controversial topics, or call-to-actions (for example go to the link in bio!) But today I wanted to specifically talk about engagement groups. You might have heard of them before, they are even called as engagement PODS, DM groups, but it's all the same. There are 2 big types of engagement groups: on Instagram and on Telegram. Both have their pros and cons. Let's start with Instagram DM groups this week, and next week we are going to discuss Telegram since it's quite a topic. Why should you use engagement groups in the first place? Or maybe you should avoid them? As I already said they can help you boost your engagement rates by having more likes and comments on your posts. In theory, by getting other accounts to quickly engage with your content, the Instagram algorithm will be tricked into thinking your post is high quality. These likes don't come from bots, and if you are using the right groups they actually come from the perfect people! On Instagram, there can be 32 people in a DM group, usually from the same niche and with the same following, which counts as really good engagement for the algorithm. What are the downsides? This is really, REALLY time-consuming! You can easily spend hours PER ACCOUNT doing these groups a day. You can't just copy and paste the same comment every time without getting banned. Only 32 members. They need to be really active to engage with your posts as soon as you drop it. (Telegram groups can have thousands of people) Now that you know the pros and cons, you can make the decision if you want to use them. If yes, how do you get in one? Just like in real life and business, having the right connections and good networking skills can help you. If you have a great page and a good following at least around 700-1000 followers you can be invited to one of them, but if it doesn't happen don't worry. There is a great trick to actually get yourself into a DM group with bigger accounts than yours! Just simply reach out to people in your niche and ask them if they are interested in supporting each other and growing together. Don't give up after the first 10 "no"!     Wealth Wednesday #6 How to recognize a real profile?

    15 Jul, 2020 •
    How to recognize a real Instagram account? Everyone is an expert today on social media, and looking at their profile full of rented cars and 10K followers can be really convincing. But how do you know if their following is real and engaging? I think you have seen it many times. Everyone can rent a nice apartment for a day to film hours of content, and bring their rented cars there as well. Also the 10.000 follower mark is really important, since this is the point where the number is no longer written out, but has the "K" letter. 10K baby! Also at 10K you can be verified and get the blue badge, and add the swipe up feature to your stories which is a really amazing tool! But let's talk about that later when we learn more about growing your IG and getting more engagement. 1. Engagement rate What is Engagement Rate(ER)? It is calculated as total engagement (likes and comments) divided by total followers, multiplied by 100. There are percentages for average engagement rates, for more see the pictures. However anything over 10% is considered really good. There are websites to check it to save the hustle, for example I use phlanx.com. 2. Follower demographics Yes, not the follower count, but the demographics. Who is likely to make more money? Someone with 11k followers from third-world countries, or an account who has 2400 loyal, engaging followers who watch stories, and message them. I would put my money on the second option. There are free tools to check if an account has fake followers, but you can do it too. Just check if their followers look real, fake accounts usually: have very few posts, no stories/profile picture, generic/no bio, follow thousand of people,have meaningless name. What is the problem? The main problem today on Intagram is if someone wants to promote their services/product they will usually pay (unrealistically) more for someone with the bigger follower count. I wish a lot of people would read through this point and could save a few $! Heck maybe hundreds of dollars.. I can register an account, post 15 pictures and buy 15k fake followers and be a guru by the end of the night, but I dont want to scam anyone. If you are looking for someone to product your services or brand, make sure you analyze their profile! Check engagement rates, their followers, how many people they are following, look at the comments and whether she/he is responding to them. Please dont pay for zero result and waste your money! Hope I could help you! Have a beautiful rest of your day!.
